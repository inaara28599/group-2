About Me

My name is Riley Ramirez. I am currently a second-year multimedia student at the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism. Before coming to Berkeley, I earned my bachelor's degree in Environmental Studies from the University of Washington in 2024. I cover climate and environment with a focus on policy, environmental justice and conservation. Occasionally, I also write about sports.

Born and raised in Los Angeles, I am a passionate Dodgers and Rams fan. I watched almost every single NFL game last season. In my free time, I enjoy playing guitar, cooking, spending time outdoors, thrifting, hanging out with my friends and family, and picking up random hobbies just to prove to myself that I can do them. I am also an avid TV and movie watcher, and love to talk pop culture.
